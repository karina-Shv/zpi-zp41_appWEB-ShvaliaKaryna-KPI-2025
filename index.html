Cat Cafe in Kyiv

The concept

Cat Cafe is a theme cafe where patrons can enjoy beverages and snacks while spending time with cats in a relaxed and cozy environment. We combine your 2 favorite things: coffee and cats. About the benefits:

  1. Cat cafés offer therapeutic benefits, including reduced stress, improved mood, and a calming environment.
  2. They provide a safe, engaging space where cats can socialize and potentially find forever homes.
  3. Cat cafe is comfort hub for cat lovers to meet, share experiences, and form friendships over a shared interest.
  4. Cats in cafés are exposed to human interaction regularly, helping them become more sociable and adoptable if the café facilitates adoptions.
  5. By housing rescue cats and facilitating adoptions, cat cafés raise awareness about responsible pet ownership and the importance of adopting rather than buying pets.

Our stars

Luna

With her long, wonderful fur and striking blue eyes, carries herself like royalty. She’s selective about who gets her attention, often walking away with an elegant flick of her tail. But if you manage to impress her (a feather toy usually does the trick), she’ll shower you with playful antics and grace.

Pudding

Soft and fluffy with a coat as golden as sunshine, Pudding is the ultimate snuggle bug. She will climb into any available lap and settle in for a nap, purring like an engine. She has a sweet tooth for cat treats and might paw at your pocket if she suspects you have some.

Shadow

A quiet cat with a small white patch on his chest, Shadow prefers the corners and shadows of the café. He’s a bit timid around strangers but warms up quickly to a patient hand. Once you earn his trust, he’ll nuzzle you endlessly and even bring you his favorite toy.

Mochi

A sleek silver tabby with bright green eyes, Mochi is always the first to investigate new visitors or toys. He has a knack for squeezing into the tiniest spaces and loves perching high up to watch the world below. Despite his adventurous spirit, he`s surprisingly gentle when approached for cuddles.

Miso

A chunky orange tabby with a penchant for trouble, Miso is the café’s resident prankster. He loves knocking over cups, stealing pens, and ambushing other cats during playtime. His antics make everyone laugh, and his goofy charm is irresistible to visitors.
